Services

We have the most creative team with more than 15+ Years’ experience in organizing the memorable weddings. We aim to be one roof for your needs. Below are a list of services that we provide.

Venue design and decor management

Right from conceptualization, planning, coordination till execution we take care of everything that is needed to make the venue presentable and suitable for your occassion. The design, décor, lights, productions, props, welcome boards etc. shall be as per your preference and liking for your dream wedding.

Conceptualization Planning and Co-ordination

For any event to be successful its important to envisage how do we want the event to look like. Conceptualizing your dream and putting it in a workable format is something which is vital to get the desired event. We also plan the entire sequence and requirements for the event to ensure that there are no goof ups on the special day. We coordinate with the right person for the job to ensure proper execution.

Entertainment and Artist Management

Our entertainment team shall provide a complete range of entertainers and artists like international bands, special acts, dance troupes to add glamour to your party.

Choreographer

From a professional dancer in your family to an amateur trying to struggle with his steps is always fun and loads of laughter during the practice sessions and sangeet. Our choreographers are sure to train you to beat the stage fear. We also conceptualize the entire sangeet event with the relevant theme and sequence the activities with proper time management.

Music Management

Music is a very integral part of indian culture. Be it any occasion, music creates an ambience. From traditional music to a Bollywood masala we shall arrange your desired team to perform at the wedding.

RSVP, Guest accommodation and Logistics

We also take care of arrangements for guests coming from outside the city with respect to their accommodation, food, transport so that they feel comfortable in the city.

Invitations and wedding stationery

Be it a relative who lives miles away or a pal who sits right next to you in your workplace, you definitely want all of them to be with you on your special da nd creatives so that they can be with you to grace the occasion.

Vendor management

Be it any event, there are many teams to work with. It requires continuous follow ups and quick action plans in case of last minute change. Instead of you getting involved or your chacha and mama taking the pain of managing them we step in to deal with all the vendors so that you and your family can completely enjoy the occasion. Right from vendor negotiation to getting things done within the budget, timeliness of activities, we take care of all the communications that needs to be done with the vendors.

Trousseau packing and wedding gifts

Sometimes its beautiful to give your guests a small gift to remember the wedding. We customize beautiful packaging of gifts and trousseaus to make It look charming.

Make up artist

Well we do believe a bride needs nothing other than her natural happy glow but a few strokes of brush will just help you in enhancing your beauty. We have one of the fine artists to make you look your best on your important day. We shall also arrange a trial with the artist for your comfort.

Mehendi

Traditionally mehendi is applied to de-stress the bride and groom from the wedding hustle, due to its cooling and medicinal properties. We use only 100% organic Mehendi that gives you the required medicinal properties with of course pretty hands and feet.

Photography

We believe in making memories and leaving a mark. Capturing a perfect candid moment, your graceful moves on the dance floor, your radiant smiles during haldi to emotional moments during wedding, the pictures will definitely be worth a thousand words.

Caterers

Indians don't let their guests go famished! We have chefs who shall cater cuisines ranging from Authentic Indian to continental and Chinese. Top notch quality food, cleanliness, best service and presentation is our promise which we shall deliver. We offer food tasting services before the event.
