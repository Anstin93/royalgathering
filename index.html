Our Story

Swati Narayan, Founder

Swati Founded “The Royal Gathering” in 2019. Academically she is a Chartered Accountant and has worked for a global big 4 company. But due to her inclination for events, decor and weddings she decided to leave her MNC job to pursue her passion. She started gaining experience in Events by working with event companies in Mumbai on various projects and has executed many large scale events single handedly. Post gaining event experience she decided to start her own dream event management company. Being from a professional background she bring the same approach and maturity while execution and dealing with clients.
